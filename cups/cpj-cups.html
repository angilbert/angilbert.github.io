Cups and peas puzzles

This variation on the tilt theme requires you to capture all the peas in cups of matching colour. An empty cup will always rotate to catch a pea, but will also drop it again, at the first opportunity. So re-arranging the peas is one half of the challenge and synchronising their capture is the other. Original applet by Graham, making use of the same generic class files as the magnetic block puzzles.

Your challenge is to fill all the cups simultaneously with peas of their respective colour. Unoccupied cups will always rotate to catch a pea if possible, but will also drop a pea if the path is clear. Black cups are spare, thay do not have matching peas.

Aim

Fill all the cups simultaneously with peas of their respective colour.

Controls

Use the Menu drop-list to select a puzzle.
Use Actions/Save to record a move sequence.

Keys

Use the cursor keys or tilt buttons to tilt the tray.
Use space to restart the current puzzle.

Puzzle index

No.3: No walls: No walls and no black cups to help you.
No.4: The big L: Harder than it looks.
