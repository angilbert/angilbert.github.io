Pathfinder
This puzzle was inspired by the NASA expedition to Mars called "Pathfinder". Large inflated balloons protect the Pathfinder while is bouces over the Mars surface to its final descent. Can you help the Pathfinder reach its goal?

This program requires a Java-enabled browser.  

Aim
Roll the gray Pathfinder piece to its goal at the south-east of the board, while avoiding the red obstacles.

Controls
Click the mouse on the board to start the puzzle.
Use next to select the next puzzle.
Use reset to restart the puzzle.
Use previous to select the next puzzle.
Use challenge drop-list to select a puzzle.

Movement
Use cursor keys to roll the gray piece, 
or use "N", "E", "W", "S" keys.
Use undo or "U" key or Ctrl+z  undo a move.
Use redo or "R" key to redo a move.
Use show solution to see the shortest solution from the start.

Here are two digital images of pathfinder puzzle, illustrating its original mechanical form, as devised by Oskar for IPP 20 in 2002. The neat spherical indents on the board surface provide a perfect control for the roll-motion.
