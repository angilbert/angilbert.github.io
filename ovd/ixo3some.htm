Oskar's threesome maze

You and two friends are stuck in a building. The three of you want to move to other room(s). Can you work together reaching your goal?

Sample puzzle
click below to play

This program requires a Java-enabled browser.
 

Aim
Manouvre the three red dots onto their blue targets (circles). There are colored markers on the floors of most rooms. If you position a red dot on a coloured marker, then doors of matching colour will open. Notice that if you want to open two doors of the same colour then two red dots should be on matching markers.

Controls
Use select maze drop-list to select a puzzle
Use reset to restart the puzzle.

Movement
Move a red dot by dragging it with the mouse or 
by using the tab and cursor keys
