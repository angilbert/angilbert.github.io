The Stepping Stone Mazes

NON-PUBLIC PAGE
This is currently a non-public page. Please do not distribute or share links to this page.
Begin each maze on the left-most stepping stone (marked with a white-square) and try to reach the right-most stepping stone (marked with an arrow). You may only step between adjacent stones, along the black lines. Also, you may not make U-turns, that is, you can't go back to the stone you just came from. So far, this is pretty simple, but here's where things get tricky. You must follow this pattern: Step on three stones of the same color, then switch to a different color. Step on three stones of that new color, then switch again. Keep repeating this rule, visiting exactly three stones of the same color, and then changing color.

See also: Bob Abbott's memo to GAMES Magazine (sent 09 September 2011)

Aim
Cross the grid, from left to right, taking exactly three steps on one color stepping-stone, and then switching to a different color. You can (and will) revisit the same group of stones several times enroute to the goal.

Controls
Use the puzzle drop-list to select a puzzle.
Use mouse clicks or cursor-keys for movement.
Use restart (action) or the space key to reset.

Movement
Use the cursor keys to move or click on a stepping stone.
Press "u" to undo recent moves (back as far as the last color change).
