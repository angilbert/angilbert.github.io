Tri-cube
In 1983-ish I discovered isometric paper. Wow! great stuff (much more exciting than graph paper). This was the era I started to dabble with structure and self-imposed rules. Note no dead-ends here (loops only), all four paths along each edge are utilised, and all grid 'intersections' are utilised in at least one direction.

From left-hand hexagon to right-hand hexagon.
