Pre-historic pipes
The pipe maze in the Maze gallery is much more elegant, but these early designs are some of the first to include two particularly strong structural features; no internal dead-ends plus back-to-back T-junctions throughout (one freely chosen T-junction pre-determines a second, which then becomes a challenge to weave into the maze). Such features can thoroughly fool those people who think they can second guess my decision process.
