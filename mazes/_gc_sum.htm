Sum trouble
From 0 to 9 using a continuous sequence of valid arithmetic sums where the result of one sum is used to start the next. For instance:

0 + 7 – 2 =  5     8   – 4 – 1 =  3  
   5  + 6 – 3 =  8     3  + 6 = 9

Move one symbol at a time around the nine black islands. You can re-use the symbols, but not in the same sum (which effectively means never step backwards). Solved it? Now try 9 to 0.
