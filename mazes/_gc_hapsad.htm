Happy-Sad
In this maze you can step only to a face that is looking directly at you. Start on the top happy face and step any distance in a row or column to a sad face. Then alternate happy, sad, happy, sad... etc. The aim is to reach the top sad face.

For example, the only valid first step is to the sad face with the red nose. There are then three possible moves (happy faces with red-noses) for the second move. After that you are on your own!
