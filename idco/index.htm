Idle Complexities is the commercial arm of the clickmazes puzzle brand. Clickmazes is a free access website offering a wide range of novel home-brew puzzle and mazes by hobby-designer Andrea Gilbert. Clickmazes has been online since 1997 bringing endless fun and puzzlement to everyone and anyone with a java-enabled browser.

Idle Complexities was formed in 2013 to help bring clickmazes puzzles to a wider commercial audience.
