The BIG-wriggle 2009 challenge!

click below to start


  

Aim
Drag the wriggly worms around the maze until you can release the blue wriggler through the light-blue gateway. You can drag by head or tail and it does not matter which end exits first (the head is marked with eyes, but this is entirely cosmetic).

Controls
Use restart (action) or the space key to reset.

Movement
Use the mouse to drag the wrigglers by their head or tail.

Recommended play
Try expanding to full-screen for a really BIG wriggle!

Can you solve the BIG-Wriggle? If so ... look out for the secret code that will pop-up, note it down and email it to me. I'll add your name to the solvers list below.

The Big Wriggle solvers list (started October 2009): Tom Jolly, Nick Baxter, Bob Henderson
Record holder: Nick Baxter (51 moves).
