ABRACADABRA maze

Hocus-pocus-obliviate-wordiness... it looks like someone cast a spell on this grid to make magical words hard to spell. To remove the spell you have to work out a way to spell the most magical word of all. Good luck!

 

Aim

Starting at the top A and finishing at the bottom A spell the magic word ABRACADABRA.

You can step any distance in a straight-line (vertical or horizontal) between letters.

Controls

Click on the grid to select the next letter.
Restart:

Hint

Letters can be used more than once.
