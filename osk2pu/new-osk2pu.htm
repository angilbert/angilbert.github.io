Oskar's parallel universe maze

You are standing in a room and wish to reach a goal in a different room. The same is the case for your alter-ego in a parallel universe. Is it possible to satisfy both your ego and your alter-ego at the same time?

Move both red balls to their targets. There are coloured markers on the floors of most rooms. If you position either red dot on a coloured marker then the doors of matching colour in the other universe will open.
