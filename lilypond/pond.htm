Lily-pond

Explore the serene world of the lily-pond and its surrounding water-gardens. Tiptoe across the delicate lily-pads, gather seed-pods, grow new lily-pads and master the natural lifecycle of the lily to delve deep into the watery wonderland. A route-finding, resource-management puzzle-game with a novel core mechanic.

This game makes extensive use of PSNext features, including level select, canvas sprites, smoothscreen (zoom) and checkpoints. Some familiarity with the puzzlescript UI is assumed and not repeated here. Suffice to say, PS games are best played on desk/laptop with a keyboard. If you know the PS UI well and feel brave enough to play on mobile I recommend you click "play full window" first.

Development notes

This puzzle concept was conceived late summer 2024 and remains in active development. To date (April 2025) there are six chapters close to finalised and two more planned (this page currently offers the first four chapters only). Later chapters will focus on the various "rules of five", the first of which is introduced in the chapter 4.

Each chapter (level) has been woven together from a large collection of small discrete puzzles which were individually hand-designed to showcase different aspects of the puzzle mechanic. You beat a level by collecting at least three rare Andreana lily blooms (each chapter contains five) and some are more challenging than others. The ultimate goal is to find all twenty, spread across the four available chapters.

The game has a restricted view centered on the player (which looks better on small screens). This gives the game a little bit of a dungeon-crawl feel and means you may occasionally need to explore beyond the visible boundary to deduce the correct way forward. Undo is your friend! Checkpoints are provided to track progress through each chapter. They only trigger on the first visit and remain visible once triggered.
