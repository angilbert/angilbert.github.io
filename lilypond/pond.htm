Lily-pond

Explore the serene world of the lily-pond and it's surrounding water-gardens. Tiptoe across the delicate lily-pads, hunt for seed-pods, grow new lily-pads and master the natural lifecycle of the flora and fauna to delve deep into the watery wonderland. A route-finding, resource-management puzzle-game with a novel core mechanic.

This puzzle concept was conceived in the summer of 2024 and has been in incremental development since then. To date (March 2025) there are six chapters close to finalised and two more planned. This page currently offers the first four chapters only.

Development notes

This game makes extensive use of PSNext features, including level select, canvas sprites, smoothscreen (for zoom and movement) and also checkpoints. Each chapter (level) is effectively a collection of discrete puzzles (typically 6-10) which must be tackled in the right order to fully beat the level. Each chapter introduces one new aspect of the core mechanic and methodically explores its puzzling potential.

The chapters (levels) are not "open-world" but different routes forward will often present themselves (especially in later chapters), part of the challenge is then deducing the correct route forward. The game is restricted to a 13x13 view centered on the player (primarily to improve mobile support) so occasionally you may need to explore a little to fully scope the next goal, but the restricted view is never deliberately exploited to obfuscate the next goal.

Checkpoints serve two purposes, firstly they allow you to revert back to a trusted state on the solution path. Secondly they offer a portal to skip you forward to the next checkpoint (hint: search in the reeds). At the moment you can also exit onto "the lawns" and re-enter the level near other checkpoints. You cannot carry seeds-pods through portals or across the lawns.
