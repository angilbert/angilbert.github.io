Odd-one-out Explanation
 1  b The mirror image is on the right, but it is on the left in all the others.
2 c It is an image of a left hand, all the rest are right hands.
3 a Opposite sides of the dice formed don't add up to seven, all others form proper dice.

5 e All the others can also be read backwards as proper words.
