Rolling-block colour-zone mazes

A black block, made of two black cubes stuck together, sits on a coloured grid. Your challenge is to roll the black block to its target position, which is marked with a small white square.

There is only one rule... the block must always sit entirely within one colour-zone.

The block starts on one end (vertical) and must also finish vertical. See the diagram on the right to illustrate.

In the applet below you will see a birds-eye view of the board. The block is not displayed in 3D, in effect you see only its shadow.

Aim

Roll the 2x1 black block to the white square.

Controls

Click Restart to load first maze or reload current maze.
Click Previous/Next to switch between mazes.

Movement

Use the cursor keys to roll.

Keys

n - next maze
p - previous maze
r - restart current maze
