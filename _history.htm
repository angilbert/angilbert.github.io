History

Also: for front page news and links from previous months see my old news page.

25/10/97
GJN(e)M 'launched'. Main points of interest being several mazes presented as GIF images, and the blackbox applet. HTML constructed with a wysiwyg editor, rather messy. No frames.

07/12/97
Frames introduced for the maze gallery and odd-one-out page. More mazes and odd-one-out puzzles added (plus solutions). New tile puzzle applet added (including 40 GIF files). Wysiwyg HTML editor abandoned; most HTML tidied or rewritten.

25/01/98
New maze catagory introduced, with two new mazes: Celtic and Pipes. 3D tilt maze and knight's tour applets added. Colour scheme switched to basically black-on-white throughout with a personal background texture. HTML fully tidied and restructured into directories.

19/02/98
Two more 3D tilt mazes added, plus a 2D version introduced, with two more mazes of its own. Tile puzzle rewritten to draw its own graphics (much faster to load). HTML maze added plus a links page, history page and applet information page.

22/04/98
Tilt Puzzles introduced and 2D/3D tilt mazes reworked to make use of the new base classes (all tilt objects now exhibit a reasonable slide/roll motion). Three more tile puzzle tile-sets added.

24/03/99
Just lots of new mazes in maze gallery.

09/10/99
Relaunch of the 2D tilt mazes, with lots of new mazes and an extensive range of multi-goal mazes. Small fixes and enhancements to other Java. One or two new mazes in the maze gallery.

08/12/99
Rolling-block colour-zone mazes (applet) introduced.

12/03/00
Hysteresis mazes (applet) and marble tilt mazes (applet) introduced. Code for 2D tilt mazes revamped to avoid repaint and reload problems.

04/06/00
GJN(e)M rebadged clickmazes (www.clickmazes.com). Maze of life (applet) added, plus a write-up on the development of NEWS and RULES, including colour-box puzzles and link to Grahams lattice puzzles. A couple of updates to the maze gallery.

03/07/00
Plank puzzles added. Applet by Graham Rogers.

13/07/00
New look page launched (design by Brett). Major update to maze of Life page.

13/08/00
Maze of life page updated with findings provided by Carl Hoff (lots of extra goals, analysis, and solutions).

01/10/00
Oskars Dragon maze added, plus updated plank puzzles page.

26/11/00
Link to Graham's interactive maze applet added in the main maze gallery. A little tidying.
