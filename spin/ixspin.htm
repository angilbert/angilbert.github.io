HexaRoll (spin mazes)

HexaRoll is a spinning puzzle-maze devised by M. Oskar van Deventer and implemented in applet form by Graham. Spin the hexagonal board until the red ball comes to rest in the yellow area. Click on the image to the right to try Oskar's full size puzzle, or first try the smaller warm up puzzle below.

Mini-HexaRoll
Spin the board until the ball comes to rest in the yellow target area.

Movement
Use the spin buttons to rotate the board. Note, the board will not spin while the ball is in motion.

Controls
Click on Restart to reset.

The photo on the right shows a mechanical version of Mini-HexaRoll implemented by M. Oskar van Deventer. It should be held vertical on a horizontal surface and rolled gently left and right.
