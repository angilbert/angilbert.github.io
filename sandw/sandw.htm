The sandwich monster

You are a green monster and your goal is to consume all the available sandwiches. Your body structure is alien to say the least... you have a free-floating head and tail that seem curiously independant of each other. Shockingly they may even be in disjoint parts of your body!

Use the cursor keys to drive the head of the monster. Reaching a sandwich will logically require stretching out towards it, but for that to happen your tail must be able to shrink in the same direction. Head and tail can interact in a very simple way, so master that bodily function first... and then head for those sandwiches.
