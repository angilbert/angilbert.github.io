Lock & Pin

A short sokoban inspired puzzle game loosely based on the theme "chalk and cheese". Objects that look similar, behave identically and share a simple goal. Why then... do they feel so contrary?

Made for: Thinky Puzzle Game Jam No.3 (theme "chalk and cheese")
